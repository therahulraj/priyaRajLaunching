*WE ARE UNSTOPPABLE NOW*

Why we always point out that a woman is weak, tortured, teased, she can't do this, she can't do that, and specially most common word, she is a "ABLA NAARI (अबला नारी)". But now we are no more "ABLA NAARI" but "ABLE NAARI" as we are able to do all kind of works in every kind of situation and in every kind of place.

A woman is the full circle. Within her is the power to create, nurture and transform. Woman has shouldered equal responsibilities with men.

And Yes,
Women's role has changed tremendously and is making its greatest impact in our society today. Many years ago, women's contribution to society was limited and controlled by men. But now women are standing tall and are playing a major role in many important areas. Women's role has changed a lot at an accelerating rate and have part in areas such as Politics, Professional Training Job, Medicine, Business and Law. Women's impact is simply due to the fact that they are now educating themselves. They are now concerned with the improvement of their own abilities. The women have left the secured domain of their home and are now in the battlefield of life, fully talented and are breaking the glass ceiling.

But as one swallow does not make a summer, the fact of some women occupying top positions does not make the development process broad-based, equitable and inclusive. Clearly, much more needs to be done. And for these things, we celebrate only one day as women's day. But, we are special, we are unique, we are us, we are women, and we don't need that one day.

So I want to put a spotlight on women in the changing world of work. It's time to bridge the gender gap and recognize women's unpaid care work. The journey of emancipation of women has crossed many milestones. But affirmative action is required for women to play their rightful role in society. The task ahead may be long and torturous. But let us make a beginning immediately.

So change the way you look at a woman's success. SHE IS UNSTOPPABLE NOW.
